Use the arrow keys or WASD to move.
Press B to place a bomb. 
Press Q or type 'quit' in the command palette to exit.

. is an empty tile
# is a wall
O is a door to another room
Numbers represent bombs (and how long until they explode)
* is an exploding tile

X is an enemy

v is armor
$ makes your bombs radius bigger
~ makes your bombs fuse shorter
B is an extra bomb
H gives you health
